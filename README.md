# Pizza Hut Sales Analysis 
Problem Statement :
The objective is to explore sales data to identify key metrics such as total revenue, popular pizza types, peak ordering hours, and category-wise performance, 
helping improve business strategy and operations.

Overview :
This project analyzes pizza sales data using SQL to uncover insights into revenue trends, customer ordering behavior, and product performance. 
The analysis is performed on relational datasets to support data-driven decision-making.

Key Highlights
Calculated total orders and revenue and identified top-performing pizzas based on quantity and revenue.
Analyzed peak order hours and customer preferences.
Performed joins across multiple tables for comprehensive analysis.
Applied window functions for cumulative revenue and advanced insights.

Key Insights : 
A small number of pizzas contribute significantly to overall revenue.
Customer orders are concentrated during specific time periods.
Certain sizes and categories are more popular among customers.

Outcome
Enabled identification of high-demand and high-revenue products and provided insights for better pricing and promotional strategies.
